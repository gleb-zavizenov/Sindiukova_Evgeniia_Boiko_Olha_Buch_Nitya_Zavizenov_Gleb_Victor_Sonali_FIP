THE MORE YOU KNOW, THE STRONGER YOU BECOME

HIV (human immunodeficiency virus) is a virus that attacks a person’s immune system. With proper treatment, HIV-positive people can live like regular people and be an equal part of society. However, without medical treatment, HIV can turn into AIDS (acquired immune deficiency syndrome).

Hello, my name is Treatment

And I am a combination of drugs that prevents the spread of HIV in a human body and keep the amount of virus at a low level. I usually call myself Antiretroviral Treatment. I don’t cure the virus but keep him locked in the cell. Sometimes, I can be so powerful that the virus is not able to affect the person’s health, and the risk of transmitting HIV becomes a ZERO.
